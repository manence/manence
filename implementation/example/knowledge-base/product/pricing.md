# Lumo pricing

- **Starter**, €9/month. The basics.
- **Pro**, **€29/month** since 2026-07-01. Includes export and integrations.

> History: Pro was €19 before 2026-07-01; it moved to €29 when export and integrations were added. We keep the record rather than overwrite it.

This legible pricing (two plans, no quotes) backs the "simple premium" [positioning](positioning.md), the opposite of [Rival X](../competitors/rival-x.md).
