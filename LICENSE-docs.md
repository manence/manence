# Documentation license (doctrine)

The **doctrine** of Manence — that is, the following markdown files:

- `README.md` and `README.fr.md`, `QUICKSTART.md` and `QUICKSTART.fr.md`, `Manifesto.md` and `Manifesto.fr.md`, `CHANGELOG.md`
- the whole `concept/` folder (including `concept/research/`)
- `implementation/Spec.md`, `implementation/Implementation.md`, `implementation/index.md`
- `assets/` (diagrams, images) and, by default, any file of the repository not covered by the MIT license of [LICENSE](LICENSE) (including `CONTRIBUTING.md` and this file)

is published under **Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)**.

Full text: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/legalcode

## What it means

**You may**: read, share, quote, translate, and adapt this doctrine — including inside a company, for internal use — as long as you credit the source.

**You may not**: exploit this text commercially — resell it, fold it into a paid course, a book, or a commercial product, with or without rebranding. For a commercial license: contact via [manence.ai](https://manence.ai).

**Expected attribution**: "Manence, Alexandre Noto, manence.ai", with a link to the canonical repository ([github.com/manence/manence](https://github.com/manence/manence)).

## What this license does NOT cover

The **code and templates** are under the **MIT** license (see [LICENSE](LICENSE)): `implementation/mos/` — the default MOS: identity files, skills, hooks, the startup ritual — and `implementation/example/`.

The intended consequence: **everything you copy into your project is yours, unconditionally.** Your CLAUDE.md, your skills, your templates are yours, commercial use included. The non-commercial restriction applies only to the framework's doctrine itself.
